Ideal Science and Technology Aiming Research Council, known as ISTARC, stands as the most distinguished co-curricular activities club at Ideal School and College. Established in 2001, ISTARC has continually fostered a passion for science among students through a rich array of engaging events.

At ISTARC, we believe in nurturing young minds through innovative and thought-provoking competitions. Our events cater to various interests and skill sets, ranging from intense group challenges to individual showcases of talent. Participants in our group events collaborate to solve complex problems, demonstrating teamwork and ingenuity. Solo competitors showcase their expertise in fields such as mathematics, physics, biology, and digital arts, among others.

Each year, ISTARC hosts a diverse lineup of events designed to challenge and inspire participants. From traditional Olympiads in sciences to cutting-edge digital art competitions and interactive quizzes, our events encourage critical thinking, creativity, and a deep appreciation for knowledge.

Moreover, ISTARC provides a platform for students to demonstrate their skills beyond academics. Events like Sci-fi Documentary and Instant Sci-fi Story Writing allow participants to explore futuristic concepts and storytelling, while competitions like Robo-Soccer and Gaming merge technology with recreational challenges.

As we continue to evolve, ISTARC remains committed to fostering a vibrant community of learners and innovators. Our events not only celebrate academic excellence but also cultivate lifelong skills such as communication, leadership, and problem-solving. Through these experiences, students gain invaluable insights and forge lasting connections.

Join us at ISTARC and embark on a journey where creativity meets knowledge, and where every participant is empowered to discover their true potential.
